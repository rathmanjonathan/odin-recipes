How to make Scrambled Eggs

Recipe Description

Creamy eggs that will ignite your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon sour cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chives

Steps:

  1. Mix together the sour cream and eggs in a small bowl. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and mix well.
  2. Melt the butter in a nonstick pan over medium-low heat. Add the eggs and gently scramble. Remove from the heat, sprinkle with the chives and serve.
